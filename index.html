About Game

Rainfall is an atmospheric 2D pixel adventure game set in a grim, dark high-fantasy universe. Master fast isometric movement, explore beautifully detailed retro environments, and uncover forgotten lore as you fight to survive a brutal world of steel and rebellion.

The Art & Making Of

Building a grim, dark high-fantasy world requires meticulous precision.

Crafted Pixel Art

Every environment, character frame, and blade of grass in Rainfall is crafted using dedicated 2D pixel art techniques, focusing on atmospheric tilesets.

Isometric Mechanics

Features smooth 4-way isometric movement blueprints and custom physics to bring the dense, dangerous terrain to life.

Modern Dev Pipeline

Our pipeline bridges classic retro aesthetics with modern mechanics—taking ideas from raw concept sketches to detailed sprite sheets animated frame-by-frame.
