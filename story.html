THE STORY

The Frogspawn Chronicles Anthology

Narrator: "Gather close, and listen to the tale of the Great Mire... back before the waters turned to ash, and when the curse of the Golden Tree Amulet was finally broken."

For centuries, the wetlands thrived in quiet prosperity. The inhabitants whispered of a legendary, long lost relic known as The Golden Tree Amulet. A near mythical artifact of immense power, it was said to anchor the lifeforce of the swamplands itself, and even possess the ability to bring life back from the dead.

But where there is light, shadows emerge...

To celebrate the birth of their unborn heir, the Frog King and Queen held a grand coronation. Among the guests was the Toad King, the Frog King's closest ally. But looking upon the unborn frogspawn, the Toad King’s heart curdled with bitter jealousy.

Desperate to end the Royal Frog bloodline and bring upon the age of Toads, the Toad King struck a dark pact with a treacherous, mystical Crow. Together, they cast a devastating curse upon the unborn spawn: the exact moment the child will come of age to take the throne, they would fall into an endless slumber.

Upon discovering the plot, the Frog King banished his former friend into the wastes forever. But the damage was done. The Crow shattered the Amulet that caused the curse, breaking it into six fragments. The Crow, keeping one for itself, scatters the remaining five pieces among the grand leaders of the wetlands. Infected by the shattered artifact's shadow magic, these once noble guardians and champions fell into corruption, becoming worshippers of the curse.

The Twist of Fate

Sixteen winters passed. Following the sudden death of the King, the palace prepared for a somber coronation. The hour of the curse had arrived. The heir stepped forward to claim the throne... and collapsed into an immediate endless sleep.

But the Crow and the Toad King had miscalculated.

The Queen had given birth to twins. The dark magic, designed to strike a single heir, only claimed one of the siblings. The other twin was left standing, untouched, unbroken, and fueled by a heavy sense of duty.

The Journey Ahead

Taking up sword and shield, the surviving twin must venture out into five blighted biomes to defeat the corrupted leaders, reclaiming the shattered fragments of the Amulet one by one. Only by defeating the five lost kings can you face the Crow for the sixth and final piece.

The journey will test your resolve, your stamina, and your morals. You must assemble the pieces, return to the palace, and break the curse.

But when the Amulet is finally made whole and you step back into the silent throne room to awaken your twin... the final challenge begins.

The Corrupted Leaders (Bosses)

Silas, The Sky Stalker

Beast Variant: Heron

A massive, elegant, but terrifying water bird with a razor-sharp beak. The Heron stands perfectly still in the shallow water, striking down with lightning-fast beak thrusts. Players will need to use their shield to block the downward stabs, creating a brief window where the Heron’s beak gets stuck in the wet mud, leaving it vulnerable to sword strikes.

Vespa, The Cavern Ghost

Beast Variant: Salamander

A sleek, amphibious cave dweller that can blend into the dark background using camouflage. It uses a long, whipping tongue attack (which heavily drains player stamina even if blocked) and can camouflage itself to surprise attack the player. This forces the player to use their stamina more carefully and to dodge roll instead of relying on blocking.

Gideon, The Forest Giant

Beast Variant: Stag Beetle

A massive, heavily armored forest insect with giant pincer jaws, acting as a literal wall of defense. This boss likes to directly charge at the player, however even though it is incredibly slow it is completely invulnerable to frontal attacks due to its thick exoskeleton. Running up and slashing will do next to no damage. The player must wait for the Beetle to charge, dodge roll behind and to its sides to attack its weaker points.

Scraps, The Carrion King

Beast Variant: Rat

A poison riddled rat that uses its toxic breath to weaken anyone in close proximity. This boss fights alongside other rats outnumbering the player and overwhelming them. Players must defeat the kings guards first causing the Rat King to become vulnerable to attacks.

Barnaby, The Sunken Knight

Beast Variant: Crab

The previous commander of the kings guard. The crab uses its claws to snag the player in its pincer causing heavy damage. The crabs huge claws can cause tremors creating falling rocks that can land damaging the player. Players must direct the crab to grab boulders by mistake causing the crab to damage itself allowing the player an opening to attack.

Raven, The Shroud

Beast Variant: Crow

Advisor to the Toad King and master of magic and curses. Its abilities and skills are currently unknown.

Titan, The Possessed King

Beast Variant: Toad

Once a trusted ally to the King of Frogs. Its abilities and skills are currently unknown.
